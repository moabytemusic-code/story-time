# Story Time with Ms. Erica - Scripts

## 1. Pip's Big Parade
**Tag:** Social Skills | **Age:** 4-7 | **Duration:** ~12 min

**(Intro)**
"Hello there! I’m Ms. Erica. Today we’re going to meet a little elephant named Pip. Pip has a tiny trunk but a very big heart."

**(Middle)**
"One sunny morning, the Jungle Parade was coming to town. Boom! Boom! went the drums. But Pip felt small. 'My trumpet sound is too quiet,' he whispered. 'Toot toot.'
His friend Lila the Lion said, 'Pip, every sound matters. Even the quiet ones.'
So Pip marched. He didn't try to be loud. He just tried to be Pip. 'Toot... toot...' 
And you know what? It was the perfect sound to fill the quiet spaces between the big drums."

**(Outro)**
"Pip learned that being himself was the bravest thing he could do. And you are perfect just being you, too."

## 2. Lila & The Ocean Song
**Tag:** Mindfulness | **Age:** 0-4 | **Duration:** ~15 min

**(Intro)**
"Let's take a deep breath in... and out... Imagine we are sitting on warm, soft sand."

**(Middle)**
"Lila loved the ocean. *Swish, swoosh.* The waves sang a lullaby. 
She dipped her toes in. The water was cool and sparkly.
She saw a little blue fish. 'Hello, fish,' she whispered.
The fish blew a bubble. *Pop!*
Lila closed her eyes and listened. The ocean wasn't rushing. It was breathing. Just like us.
In... and out..."

**(Outro)**
"The ocean helps us feeling calm. Whenever you feel busy, just listen for the waves inside your heart."

## 3. The Sleepy Moon
**Tag:** Bedtime | **Age:** 0-4 | **Duration:** ~20 min

**(Intro)**
Start with a slow, whispery voice.
"The sun has gone to bed... and the moon is waking up."

**(Middle)**
"Mr. Moon looks down at the world. He sees the sleepy owl. 'Hoo, hoo, goodnight.'
He sees the sleepy car in the driveway. 'Beep, beep, sleep tight.'
He sees you, snug in your bed.
One by one, the stars turn on their night-lights. Blink... blink...
The world is slow. The world is soft."

**(Outro)**
"You are safe in the moonlight. Drift away on a cloud of dreams. Goodnight..."

## 4. Leo's Lost Roar
**Tag:** Emotions | **Age:** 4-7 | **Duration:** ~10 min

**(Intro)**
"Leo was a lion with a very big problem. He opened his mouth to ROAR, but out came a... *squeak*."

**(Middle)**
"Leo felt hot. He felt grumpy. He stomped his paws. 'I want my roar!'
His mom came over. 'Leo, sometimes our roars get stuck when we have big feelings.'
She taught him the Lion Breath. 'Stick out your tongue and let it all out.'
Leo tried it. *Haaaaa.*
He didn't find his roar right away, but he found his smile."

**(Outro)**
"It's okay to feel squeaky sometimes. Your big roar will always come back when you're ready."

## 5. The Sharing Tree
**Tag:** Kindness | **Age:** 4-7 | **Duration:** ~14 min

**(Intro)**
"Deep in the forest stood the Sharing Tree. Its branches were heavy with the most delicious golden apples."

**(Middle)**
"Squirrel wanted all the apples. 'Mine!' he chattered.
But the Tree whispered, 'Happiness is bigger when you share.'
Squirrel thought about it. He gave an apple to Deer.
Deer smiled a big, wobbly smile. Squirrel felt something warm in his tummy. It felt better than eating the apple himself!
The more he shared, the more fruit the tree seemed to grow."

**(Outro)**
"Sharing spreads magic. And that magic grows right back in your heart."

## 6. Ruby's Rainy Day
**Tag:** Optimism | **Age:** 0-4 | **Duration:** ~8 min

**(Intro)**
"Pitter patter, pitter patter. Ruby pressed her nose against the glass. 'Oh no, rain!'"

**(Middle)**
"Ruby wanted to play tag. But the sky was gray.
Then she saw a puddle. A big, round, shiny puddle.
She put on her yellow boots. *Squish, squash.*
She jumped! *Splash!*
The rain wasn't ruining her day. It was making music on her roof. It was washing the flowers."

**(Outro)**
"Ruby learned that a sunny heart makes every day bright, no matter the weather."

## 7. The Magic Paintbrush
**Tag:** Creativity | **Age:** 7-9 | **Duration:** ~10 min

**(Intro)**
"Sam found an old brush in the attic. But when he painted a butterfly, it wiggled off the paper!"

**(Middle)**
"Sam painted a door on the wall. *Creak!* It opened.
He walked through into a land made of drawings.
The trees were scribbles. The sun was a big yellow swirl.
He realized he could change anything. He painted a bridge over a scary river. He painted a ladder to reach the stars."

**(Outro)**
"Your imagination is your own magic paintbrush. You can create whole worlds just by thinking."

## 8. Benny's Balloon Ride
**Tag:** Adventure | **Age:** 7-9 | **Duration:** ~15 min

**(Intro)**
"Buckle up! Benny just found a giant red balloon tied to his porch."

**(Middle)**
"Benny held the string. *Whoosh!* Up he went!
Over the rooftops, over the school. The cars looked like toys.
He floated past a cloud castle where the Cloud King lived.
He saw birds flying south and waved hello.
The world was so big and beautiful from up here."

**(Outro)**
"Benny landed softly in his backyard, but his dreams stayed high in the sky."

## 9. Detective Fox
**Tag:** Problem Solving | **Age:** 7-9 | **Duration:** ~12 min

**(Intro)**
"Mrs. Badger's blueberry pie was missing! Who took it? Detective Fox is on the case."

**(Middle)**
"Fox looked for clues. He found a blue stain on the rug.
He found a crumb near the door.
He asked the owl, 'Who, who?'
With his magnifying glass, he followed the trail... right to the Baby Bear's den.
But Baby Bear wasn't eating it. He was saving it to surprise his mom!"

**(Outro)**
"Detective Fox solved the mystery, but the surprise was the sweetest part."

## 10. The Brave Little Firefly
**Tag:** Courage | **Age:** 4-7 | **Duration:** ~8 min

**(Intro)**
"Flicker was a firefly who was afraid of the dark. 'It's too spooky!' he said."

**(Middle)**
"All his friends zoomed into the night. Flicker stayed under a leaf.
But then he heard a tiny cry. A baby beetle was lost!
Flicker took a deep breath. He turned on his light. *Glow.*
The dark wasn't empty anymore. It was filled with his light.
He guided the beetle home."

**(Outro)**
"Courage isn't about not being scared. It's about letting your light shine even when you are."

## 11. Morning Sun Salutation
**Tag:** Morning Routine | **Age:** 0-4 | **Duration:** ~5 min

**(Intro)**
"Good morning! Let's wake up our bodies together."

**(Middle)**
"Reach up high to the sky... say 'Hello, Sun!'
Touch your toes... say 'Hello, Earth!'
Wiggle your fingers like rain.
Stomp your feet like a friendly giant.
Now, put your hands on your heart. Feel it beating? *Thump, thump.*
You are ready for a brand new day."

**(Outro)**
"Take that sunshine with you wherever you go today!"

## 12. The Grumpy Cloud
**Tag:** Emotions | **Age:** 4-7 | **Duration:** ~10 min

**(Intro)**
"Gary the Cloud felt heavy and gray. He didn't want to float. He wanted to sit."

**(Middle)**
"The wind tried to push him. 'Move along, Gary!'
But Gary just grumbled. *Rumble, rumble.*
He was full of rain. He held it in tight.
Finally, he couldn't hold it anymore. He let it go. cry. *Drip, drop, pour.*
The rain fell down... and watered the thirsty flowers below."

**(Outro)**
"Gary felt light and fluffy white again. Sometimes, you just need a good cry to feel better."
